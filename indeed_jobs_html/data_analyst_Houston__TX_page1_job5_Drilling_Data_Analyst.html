About the role\n
\n
\n We are seeking Drilling Data Analysts located in Houston to provide part of the 24/7 operational support for the Corva system. The Analyst ensures that each well on Corva is ready for our customers to make real-time drilling decisions and requires a laser focus on accuracy and timeliness. The Analyst supports customers by proactively QCing all data and visuals on Corva and ensuring our clients have the tools needed to make the best decisions possible..\n
