POSITION TITLE

\n

Investments Data Management Analyst

\n

\n


POSITION LOCATION

\n

Stamford, CT

\n

\n


YOUR ROLE

\n

You will be an integral part of the Data Management Team delivering high quality data and various deliverables to our key business areas including (but not limited to) traders, portfolio management, credit research and risk. It will be imperative for you to deliver accurately and timely data to our customers who use that information to make investment decisions based on the data. You will work cross-functionally to improve data needs and deliverables, increase efficiencies/controls, and build positive relationships with our customers.

\n

\n

What you will be doing

\n
    \n
  • Collaborate with front office to set-up securities in source system accurately and timely
    • \n
  • Execute various reconciliations of our source data to ensure accuracy
    • \n
  • Prepare cash flow modeling, projections and analysis for downstream consumption
    • \n
  • You will learn our various security valuation processes and controls
    • \n
  • Continually Identify process deficiencies, implement controls and maintain documentation
    • \n
  • Participate in the continual development of a Data Governance framework
    • \n
\n
