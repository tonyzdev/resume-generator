Full job description

WisEngineering is seeking a highly motivated and experienced Process and Data Solutions Analyst to drive digital transformation and operational efficiency for our government customer. The ideal candidate will possess a strategic mindset for process optimization combined with deep technical skills in the Microsoft 365 Enterprise ecosystem, particularly the Power Platform. This role requires managing sensitive government data (CUI, PII, PHI) and includes secondary responsibilities in simple IT support.

Key Responsibilities

1. Strategic Optimization and Knowledge Management (40%)

•

Process Analysis: Evaluate current workforce strategies and existing business processes to identify gaps, inefficiencies, and opportunities for automation and optimization.

•

Strategy Development: Design and recommend data-driven strategies to improve overall organizational efficiency and workflow performance.

•

Knowledge Management: Develop and implement effective knowledge management systems (KMS) and organizational structures within SharePoint and Microsoft Teams to enhance collaboration and information sharing across the organization.

2. Digital Workflow Automation and App Development (40%)

•

Solution Design: Develop, deploy, and maintain custom solutions—including automated workflows and data-driven tools—to replace manual processes (e.g., status reporting, credit card approvals, data collection).

•

Power Platform Expertise: Utilize the Microsoft Power Platform to build solutions:

•

Developing business applications using Power Apps (Canvas and Model-Driven).

•

Creating complex, multi-step automated processes using Power Automate (Flow).

•

Data Architecture: Re-organize and consolidate numerous disparate data sources into a more coherent and sustainable data store architecture (leveraging SharePoint Lists or Dataverse).

3. Data-Driven Decision Making (10%)

•

Reporting & Analytics: Develop interactive reports, visualizations, and dashboards using Power BI to enable data-driven decision-making across leadership and operational teams.

•

Data Modeling: Perform data modeling and transformation within Power BI to ensure accuracy, coherence, and integrity of key performance indicators (KPIs).

4. System Administration & IT Support (10%)

•

Act as the primary point of contact for simple Tier 1 IT issues and hardware lifecycle management.

•

Set up, image, and issue new IT equipment (laptops, peripherals).

•

Obtain and utilize required authorities (e.g., local admin rights) to handle simple technical tasks such as software installation, driver installation, and basic troubleshooting.

•

Assist with user access issues, including password resets.

Required Qualifications and Skills

•

Proven experience developing and deploying solutions using Microsoft Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power BI.

•

Strong understanding of Microsoft SharePoint architecture for content and data management.

•

Demonstrated ability to perform business process analysis, requirements gathering, and strategic planning.

•

Must be 'clearable' and able to handle Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI), Personally Identifiable Information (PII), and Protected Health Information (PHI) in compliance with government regulations.

Highly Desired Qualifications (Plus Factors)

•

Prior experience with government systems for time tracking, financial management, or other federal/state internal applications.

•

Experience with basic system administration tasks (imaging, software deployment, Tier 1 help desk support).
