\n Posting Summary\n
\n
\n
\n Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey is seeking a Data Analyst for the School of Engineering.

Among the key duties of the position are the following:\n
\n
    \n
  • Facility modeling with 3D imaging data and building information modeling tools.
    • \n
  • Geospatial data processing.
    • \n
  • Lidar data collection and processing.
    • \n
  • Works on developing, building information models from laser scan data, writing model specifications, and collaborates with other researchers on tasks related to crowd simulation and exploration of what-if scenarios.
    • \n
  • Writes progress reports, makes presentations, and sometimes travels to collect data.
    • \n
